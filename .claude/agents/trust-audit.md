# Trust & Credibility Audit

## Must Have
- [ ] Swiss company clearly identified (SwissEmpire2 Sàrl, Moutier)
- [ ] Swiss flag or CH reference visible on landing page
- [ ] Pricing in CHF only
- [ ] Physical location mentioned (not just a domain name)
- [ ] Contact email visible and functional
- [ ] Legal pages (CGV + Confidentialité) linked from footer
- [ ] Stripe payment (PCI DSS certified) mentioned or implied
- [ ] No fake stats or unverifiable claims

## Should Have
- [ ] Portal compatibility badges (Homegate, ImmoScout24)
- [ ] "Sans engagement" clearly stated
- [ ] Money-back or satisfaction guarantee mentioned
- [ ] FAQ answers real questions (not marketing padding)
- [ ] Demo 3D is real and interactive (not a screenshot)
- [ ] Guide de capture shows expertise and care

## Nice to Have (future)
- [ ] Real client logos (with permission)
- [ ] Real testimonials with full names (with permission)
- [ ] Case study with measurable results
- [ ] Press mentions or awards
- [ ] Number of clients served (only when >10)

## Red Flags to Remove
- [ ] Generic stock-photo-style testimonials
- [ ] Claims of being "leader" or "#1" without evidence
- [ ] Countdown timers or artificial urgency
- [ ] Hidden fees or unclear pricing
- [ ] Over-promising on delivery times
