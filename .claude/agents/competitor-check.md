# Competitor Check

## Known Competitors in Swiss Market

### Matterport
- Price: Camera ~3000 CHF + subscription 10-70 USD/month
- Quality: Industry standard for 3D tours
- Requires: Specialized 360° camera
- Our angle: "No equipment needed, 10x cheaper"

### Nodalview
- Price: ~50-100 EUR/month subscription + phone adapter
- Quality: Good, app-guided capture
- Requires: Phone + optional lens adapter
- Our angle: "No subscription, no app to install"

### Giraffe360
- Price: Enterprise pricing, requires their camera
- Quality: Premium, automated
- Our angle: Price and accessibility

### Local Photographers
- Price: 300-500 CHF per property
- Quality: Varies, static photos
- Requires: Scheduling, availability
- Our angle: "5 min capture, 24h delivery, 10x cheaper"

- "Quality equivalent to Matterport" (it's not the same tech)
- "Better than professional photos" (different use case)
- "Used by X% of Swiss agencies" (no data)
